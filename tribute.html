LORD KRISHNA

WHO IS KRISHNA?

Lord Krishna, the eighth avatar of Vishnu, is the god of love, compassion, and wisdom. Born in Mathura, his life symbolizes the triumph of good over evil. He is renowned for his teachings in the Bhagavad Gita, guiding humanity on dharma, karma, and devotion.

HIS ACHEIVEMENTS

Lord Krishna's key achievements include:
Defeating Evil: He vanquished demons like Kansa and Shishupala to restore dharma.
Guiding Humanity: Delivered the Bhagavad Gita, offering timeless teachings on life and duty.
Establishing Dwarka: Created a prosperous city and protected his people.
Uniting Pandavas: Played a crucial role in the Mahabharata, ensuring justice prevailed.

HIS BELOVED "RADHARANI"

Radha, Krishna's eternal consort, symbolizes pure love and devotion. Their bond represents the soul's longing for the divine. Celebrated in art and poetry, Radha-Krishna exemplify spiritual unity and inspire devotion in Hindu traditions.
